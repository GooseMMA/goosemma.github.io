About the Game

Atlas is a multiplayer sandbox MMO set in a dark and dangerous universe. Explore worlds, build, farm, trade and fight while carving your own path through progression and discovery.

Not every world is safe. Power is earned.

Core Features

Dangerous Worlds

Exploration often leads to conflict. Safety is never guaranteed.

Sandbox Building

Shape worlds using blocks and items obtained through gameplay.

Player Economy

Trade freely with other players using in-game currency.

Progression Through Action

Some systems are discovered, not explained.

Atmosphere

Dark fantasy inspired world filled with ruins, monsters and silent danger.
This is not a safe sandbox.
