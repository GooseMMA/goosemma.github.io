Atlas Exclusive Features

World Conquest System

When you type in a world name, you don't just spawn in. New worlds contain monsters and dungeon layouts that must be cleared to make the world a "Safe Spot".

World Ownership

After clearing all monsters and defeating the boss, you can lock the world and become its owner, creating your personal territory.

Monster Killer Ranks

From E to S rank, your Monster Killer progression unlocks higher-tier content and rewards based on monsters defeated.

Guild Raids & Ranks

Guilds can participate in challenging raids to fight powerful monsters and clear dungeons within time limits, with their own E-S ranking system.

Deep Progression Systems

Dual Leveling

Separate leveling system from Monster Killer ranks, providing additional bonuses and progression paths.

Legendary Items

Obtained exclusively through Guild Raids, these rare items (only 1-3 exist) provide powerful guild-wide buffs and are held for limited time.

Item Acquisition

Special items can be purchased via IAPs or earned through monster drops, with varying drop rates from common to extremely rare.

Customization

Purchase basic clothing packs or splice them with seeds for unique appearances and combinations.

Core Features

Player Trading

Trade freely with other players using in-game currency earned through gameplay, just like in Pixel Worlds and Growtopia.

World Building

Build your worlds using blocks obtained through splicing or purchased in shop packs, creating unique environments.

Farming & Resources

Harvest resources, farm materials, and craft items to enhance your gameplay and trading capabilities.

Community Driven

Join guilds, participate in events, and collaborate with other players to unlock the full potential of Atlas.
